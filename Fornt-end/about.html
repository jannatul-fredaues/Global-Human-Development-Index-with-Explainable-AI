Why we built this

A final year project meant to prove we can ship something real.

Development statistics are usually handed over as a finished score with no visible working. We wanted to build something that keeps the working visible — a system that computes the Human Development Index and explains, in plain language, which factor moved a country's score and by how much. It's our way of showing the kind of engineering and analytical thinking we want to bring into our careers.
